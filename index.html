Developed Bioinformatics Tools & Platforms 生信工具与平台开发

BrassicaIBD & DLGP

Developed an intelligent breeding platform for rapeseed (BrassicaIBD) integrating multi-omics data. Engineered a Deep Latent Genomic Prediction (DLGP) deep learning model to accelerate modern genomic breeding efficiency.

开发了整合多组学数据的油菜智能育种平台 (BrassicaIBD)。构建了深度潜在基因组预测 (DLGP) 深度学习模型，以提高现代基因组育种效率。

PGFAT & GWASNexus

Created the Pan-Gene Family Analysis Toolkit (PGFAT), a Python-based command-line pipeline for high-throughput genomic data. Developed GWASNexus for advanced post-GWAS analysis.

创建了泛基因家族分析工具包 (PGFAT)，这是一个基于 Python 的高通量基因组数据命令行分析流程。开发了用于 GWAS 后续深度分析的 GWASNexus 工具。
